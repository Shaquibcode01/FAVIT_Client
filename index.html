The Eco Environ Foundation, founded by Dr. ULAGAMANI EDADI in the year 2021, is committed to empowering young people with earning opportunities and enhancing access to facilities in remote areas. The organization is dedicated to providing a better future for the underprivileged and the younger generation through various initiatives, including:

⋆ Funding a college student to set up an ice cream business.

⋆ Assisting people in remote villages to access essential facilities in collaboration with the state government.

Dr. Ulagumani Edadi, born and raised in Madurai, a city in the state Tamil Nadu, known as the sleepless city and famous for temples , food and especially the Madurai Malli (jasmine flower). After completing his college, he moved to North India to work for Jain irrigations – one of the pioneers in the solar and drip irrigations in India. Then he moved back to south India and worked as the Business head for KCP solar and Ace Renewtech, both being the best solar companies in TamilNadu.

Under his professional leadership, he contributed a whopping 5MW of solar ground-mount projects, more than 500 kw of solar rooftop projects and more than 900 solar pumps being installed in the state of Karnataka. All these numbers are merely an indication of the hard work, which he poured for the above company’s development.

After serving the industry for five years and gaining a vast experience in the field of renewable energy industry and expertise in Solar, Biogas, and Energy auditing. In the year 2019, he also founded Blue Bright Energy Private Limited, a company offering solar energy solutions services. not for his personal gain, but for a change, he wishes to see in reality, in the Indian subcontinent. The change he wishes to see is the development of India in pioneering the Sustainable development goals and establishing itself as the foremost leader in tackling climate change as well as global warming. He firmly believes that the change always starts from one single individual and he has the content that, he is such an individual.

Apart from professional achievements , he is a patriotic person and always wishes for the good things for the people around him. He is an embodiment of “Leadership” and he developed himself by learning from the footsteps of Dr.B.R.Ambedkar. He is an exhilarating personality and always wishes the people around him to be motivated.

In short, Dr.Ulagamani embraces the philosophy of sustainability and he believes that he can definitely make a change in this society. He always thinks of contributing to Mother nature and he started his part, in the process of revolutionizing India.
